# Microscope Objective Storage Case 🔬

## 🎯 Purpose
A robust, dust-proof storage solution designed for precision **microscope objectives**. This case provides a secure environment for high-end optics when not in use.

---

## 📦 Components
* **Case Body:** Designed for structural integrity and lens protection.
* **Plain Top Cover (Recommended):** The standard lid that ensures a perfect fit for the objective.
* **Branded Top Cover (Design Warning):** A version featuring a logo. 
* **Source Files:** **FreeCAD (.FCStd)** files are included for custom adjustments.

---

## ⚠️ Important Note on the Branded Cover (Fitment Issue)
Please be aware that there is a **design clearance issue** with the branded (logo) version of the cover:
* **Fitment Conflict:** Due to a design oversight in the internal geometry, the **Branded Cover may not close properly** when a microscope objective is inside the case. The internal structure of this specific lid may physically interfere with the objective's height or width.
* **Recommendation:** It is highly recommended to print and use the **Plain Top Cover** for functional storage. The branded version is currently provided for aesthetic reference or for those who wish to fix the clearance in the provided **FreeCAD** source files.

---

## 🛠 Assembly & Maintenance
* **Post-Processing:** Light **sanding** on the mating surfaces (where the lid meets the body) is recommended to ensure a smooth opening and closing experience.
* **Safety:** Always verify that the interior is free of 3D printing debris or dust before storing your microscope objective.
